Program Basics

What is Gaja Health?

Gaja Health is a health advocacy company focused on helping you with what matters most - overcoming challenges in your home and surrounding community to improve your health and wellbeing.

We are partnering with Anthem to support you as you use your Nutrition Life Essentials Kit benefit. Our program includes access to a team of Health Advocates - trained and experienced professionals who have been where you and your family are and whose only job is supporting you.

What makes Gaja Health different from other healthcare services or apps?

Gaja aims to personalize your experience of our medical system by giving you access to a partner to help you navigate healthcare and your own wellbeing. Most experiences with your medical care team are often too transactional. You may have experienced being paired with someone who really doesn’t understand you, despite their best intentions. Gaja values relationship building and focuses on helping you improve your health and live a happier healthy life on your terms.

Who’s eligible?

Anthem employees who are enrolled in the Anthem Nutrition Life Essentials Kit, and received our “Welcome to Gaja Health” email.

Who pays for Gaja Health?

Gaja’s services are paid for by Anthem and therefore free to eligible employees as part of your Nutrition Life Essentials Kit.

How long does the program last?

3 months / 90 days

What does the Gaja Health program include?

A Gaja Health Advocate. A real human being who wants to listen to you and your unique needs.

Your Gaja Health experience starts by getting to know a Health Advocate over a 30-40 minute phone call. Once you have created your account, use the “Schedule call” button on your home screen to schedule a call.

See ”Using Your App” below for more details.

Accessible support

Your Health Advocates are available 12 hour a day, 5 days a week. Reach out at your convenience with questions, comments or concerns you may have. Their aim is to support you living a healthier life by addressing your mind, body and soul. You can go to them with problems, questions, or support related to your goals and wellbeing.

See “Health Advocacy” below for more details.

An App made just for you

With the Gaja Health app you can access tools aimed to reduce the stress and anxiety of staying on track with a healthy lifestyle. Think of it as a Personal Assistant in the palm of your hands, ready to help with finding recipes, self-care exercises, and tips to take small steps towards reaching your health goals.

In addition to the available in-app tools, your advocates can help personalize health-related educational content to you and your family’s unique needs.

Getting Started

How do I get started?

  1. Download the App: click here for iOS or here for Android
  2. Click the “Create Account” button
    1. Enter your Anthem email address
    2. Set up your new Gaja Health password
    3. Enter your contact info
    4. Read and sign our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
    5. Enter the App
  3. Schedule your Intro call by clicking the “Schedule call” button, then:
    1. Choose a time
    2. Input your information
    3. Click “Schedule event”

How do I schedule my first call?

Use the “Schedule call” button on the Home screen in your app, which will direct you to Calendly.com. From there you simply need to:
  1. Choose a time
  2. Input your information
  3. Click “Schedule event”
  4. Check your calendar/email - you should receive a calendar invite as a confirmation.
See “How do I get started” for more details on setting your profile, or email help@gogajahealth.com if you are having trouble.

What is the Intro call for?

The Intro call is meant to help your Advocate learn more about you, your goals, and also identify things that may be getting in your way.

During the call, your Health Advocate will focus on asking questions about things like food, your LEK benefit, stress, your health, etc.

In the end, it is your choice to share whatever information you want with us, and we will meet you where you are at to focus on what is most important to you.

Our Solution - Health Advocacy

What is a ‘Health Advocate’?

A Health Advocate is a trusted professional whose job it is to support you. Think of your Advocates as a personal coach, navigator, and supporter. They will check in during your program to see how you are doing, but are also there for you when you need them. All you have to do is ask!

Our Gaja Health Advocacy team can help you with:

  1. Getting the most out of Nutrition LEK benefit dollars
  2. Help setting goals and building healthy habits
  3. Supporting you and your family in eating nutritious meals (taking care of your body), meditating (taking care of your mind), and taking care of your financial health with user-friendly budget tools and resources.

Using Your Gaja Health App

What does the Gaja Health app do?

The App has 3 main functions:

  1. Chat: use the chat to ask questions about things that are getting in your way day-to-day, especially regarding your Nutrition Life Essentials Kit benefit, your nutrition, and your goals.
  2. Search ffor recipes, self-care exercises, and educational tips on the Search page.
  3. Save rrecipes, exercises, tips, etc. that you like by clicking the heart icon.

What are the times my advocate is available to support me?

Advocacy team hours are from approximately 10 am to 10 pm eastern time, 7 am to 7 pm pacific.

What if I have an emergency?

Call 9-1-1 for serious medical emergencies. If you don’t need help right away, leave your Health Advocacy team a message and they’ll respond as soon as they’re back on-line.

What does “Budget”, cost, and $ refer to in my recipe results?

The “Budget” category and $ filter buttons allow you to sort your recipe results by relative cost. While these don’t pertain to a specific dollar amount, selecting either category is a helpful way to find recipes that use inexpensive ingredients that won’t break the bank.

I prefer phone calls - how can I set up a follow-up call with my Health Advocate?

We understand there are times when you’d prefer to hear the voice of someone you trust. If this is the case, feel free to send a message to a Health Advocate to set up a time to talk.

I’m having problems with the App - who’s my tech support?

If you have any questions or issues with the App, please email our team at help@gogajahealth.com and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
