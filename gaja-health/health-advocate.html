We're here to help you and your family live your healthiest lives

Hi there!

My name is Eduardo Jasso, I am the Director of Social Work at Gaja Health.

I wanted to let you know we are here to provide trusted support and resources to help you and your family live your healthiest lives at no additional cost to you.

In our program, you will have direct access to me and my teammates, to guide you through your chosen health journey. We are available 12 hours a day, 5 days a week to help you and your family grow and live a more stress-free, healthy life, offering support for your mental, physical, financial, and nutritional health.

We are here for you. In your first call, we will spend the time to better understand you, what you’ve been through, and where you’d like to go. Next, we’ll work together to set achievable goals and set up a Health Action Plan. After our initial calls, we will be there every step of the way for the next 3 months, to cheer you on when you’re making great progress and to be a shoulder to lean on when you’re struggling.
