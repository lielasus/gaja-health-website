How Does Gaja Health Work?

Your partners, called “Health Advocates” are from Alameda County and ready to listen to you and help with your highest priority needs.

Our trusted Health Advocates are here to help you with:

  • Housing
  • Food
  • Employment
  • Emotional support
  • Getting medical, dental, and vision services

Who is Gaja Health For?

We are offering our services at no cost to Medi-Cal Members in Alameda County. Gaja Health is built to connect you to care and support you in your needs.

If you are struggling with high stress, finding housing, putting food on the table, getting access to a doctor, or more, please click the button to apply for our program. (If you are eligible, a Gaja Health Advocate will text you.)
